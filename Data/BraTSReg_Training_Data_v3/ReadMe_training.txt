Thank you for your interest in BraTS-Reg challenge. 
This folder contains complete BraTS-Reg 2022 training data, including ground truth annotations. 

Please make sure that whenever you use and/or refer to the BraTS-Reg challenge and datasets in your manuscripts, you always cite the following paper:
[1] B.Baheti, D.Waldmannstetter, S.Chakrabarty, et al., "The Brain Tumor Sequence Registration Challenge: Establishing Correspondence between Pre-Operative and Follow-up MRI scans of diffuse glioma patients", arXiv preprint arXiv:2112.06979 (2021)

By registering for the BraTS-Reg challenge and downloading the dataset, you agree that the multi-institutional BraTS-Reg challenge dataset of anonymized and defaced subjects' pre-op and follow-up scans will only be used for the purposes of algorithmic development and evaluation, in the context of the BraTS-Reg challenge. You also agree to not redistribute the data and make no attempts to re-identify this data in any capacity.
